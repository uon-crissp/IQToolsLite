Patient data collected at routine follow ups such as vitals, opportunistic infections, WHO and WAB staging etc. is available in the tmp_ClinicalEncounters table.

This table serves as a source for the following views

1.IQC_eWAB and IQC_eWHO – Enrollment WAB and WHO stages for all patients

2.IQC_bWAB and IQC_bWHO – Baseline WAB and WHO stages for all patients

3.IQC_lastWAB and IQC_lastWHO – Last recorded WAB and WHO staging for all patients

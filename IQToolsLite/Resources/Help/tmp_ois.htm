All reported opportunistic infections are extracted from tmp_ClinicalEncounters and stored in this table for easy querying. Extra flags for OI occurrence at enrollment and baseline are added for classification. Of high interest are Tuberculosis, Pneumonia and Cryptococcal Meningitis. These are also flagged for easier identification.

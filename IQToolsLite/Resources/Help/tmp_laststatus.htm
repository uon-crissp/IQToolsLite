This table contains a list of all patient with a corresponding drop out reason and date. tmp_lastStatus applies CDC reporting rules on multiple drop out reasons as indicated below.

•If patient transferred out, then was lost, count as transferred out.

•If patient transferred out, then died, count as transferred out.

•If patient transferred out, then stopped, count as transferred out.

•If patient stopped ARVs, then died, count as stopped.

•If patient stopped ARVs, then transferred out, count as stopped.

•If patient stopped ARVs, then was lost, count as stopped.

These are captured in the CDCExitReason and CDCExitReason columns.

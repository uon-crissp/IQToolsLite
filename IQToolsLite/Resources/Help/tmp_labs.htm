IQTools exposes lab data in a friendly format with enrollment, baseline and 6 month interval classification for easy querying. tmp_Labs contains all labs recorded for all patients. Labs recorded in previous facilities and documented during initial evaluation are also included in this table. These are usually baseline and most recent CD4s.
